BarRemote for Spotify

Control Spotify playback on your Mac

or on remote Macs in your network,

using your Mac or iOS device.

BarRemote is a simple app that allows you to control your Spotify player from the menubar. It also allows you to control the Spotify player on other Macs in your network, if they are also running the BarRemote app.

Discovery of machines is automatic, and you don't have worry about IP addresses or port numbers. The apps will find themselves and connect automatically.

Control Spotify directly from the menu bar.

Launch the app and you're instantly controlling Spotify on your local machine.

Big artwork display and playback controls in the menubar.

If you prefer not to see the controls in the menu bar, just flick a switch and they'll show inside the application window.

 

Support for seeking in track.

Jump to any point in the current track.

 

Control volume, shuffle and repeat options of playback.

 

No longer need to switch to the Spotify player to see the artwork, what's playing, or change tracks.

 

It's all a click away, in the menubar.

Control remote machines

Also launch the app on other Macs in your network

and they'll automatically discover each other

and allow you to control any of them.

No configuration required.

No more walking over to the Mac

in your home that is playing Spotify

to change tracks or see what's playing.

 

No IP addresses or ports to remember.

Just select the Mac from the list.

That's it.

 

You're now controlling that machine.

How to make remote control fun

1. Fire the radio. In the office, have one machine that is playing Spotify. Using remote control anyone can skip that annoying track, or turn down the volume if that important phone call comes in.

You see, I'm all for democracy in the office.

2. For the curious. If everyone in your office is using headphones you can all peak at what each other is listening to. You obviously don't want everyone to be able to change your tracks. Just flick the switch to disable remote control of your machine. Now they can only see what you're playing.

Still won't help you with those tracks you secretly enjoy. The ones you don't want anyone to know about.

3. For the physically active. With the release of the iOS app, playback control is in your pocket. No matter where you are. No matter...
