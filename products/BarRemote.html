BarRemote for Spotify

Control Spotify playback on your local Mac

or on remote Macs in your network,

using your Mac or iOS device.

 

BarRemote is a simple app that allows you to control your Spotify player from the menubar. It also allows you to control the Spotify player on other Mac machines in your network, if they are also running the BarRemote app.

Discovery of machines is automatic, and you don't have worry about IP addresses or port numbers. The apps will find themselves and connect automatically.

from any other Mac or iOS device.

No configuration required.
